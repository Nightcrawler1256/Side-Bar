Overview

The accommodation options at Utsav combine historical legacy with contemporary features and amenities. With a total of 35 rooms, Utsav can accommodate guests with exclusive and premium services.

The suite is a world of its own, ensuring absolute privacy within an apartment provisioned with all the amenities your heart could wish for. The air-conditioned luxury suites are fitted with 32" LED TV screens, spacious wardrobes complete with lockers and luggage compartments, amenities like 24-hour Internet/Broadband facility, telephones, refrigerator, writing table, mini bar, and tea and coffee maker.
